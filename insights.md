# Trader Behavior Insights

## Methodology

The analysis involved cleaning and merging two datasets:

1. Bitcoin Fear/Greed Index
2. Hyperliquid Trader Transaction Data

Trader data was aggregated by date and merged with sentiment data to analyze behavioral patterns.

---

## Key Findings

### 1. PnL Behavior

Trader profit and loss shows greater volatility during Fear sentiment periods, indicating higher market uncertainty.

### 2. Trade Size Behavior

Trade sizes tend to increase during Greed sentiment periods, suggesting traders take more aggressive positions.

### 3. Trader Segmentation

Clustering analysis reveals three trader archetypes:

• Small trade casual traders  
• Moderate consistent traders  
• High volume high risk traders  

---

## Strategy Recommendations

1. Reduce trade size during Fear sentiment periods to limit risk.

2. Avoid excessive trading during Greed sentiment.

3. Use behavioral segmentation to apply targeted trading strategies.
